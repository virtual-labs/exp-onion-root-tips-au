### About the Experiment 

The "Isolation of Mitochondria" virtual lab, offered by Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, provides an interactive platform to understand the process of isolating mitochondria from cells using differential and density gradient centrifugation. It covers the structural and functional aspects of mitochondria, emphasizing their role in ATP production and cellular metabolism.
